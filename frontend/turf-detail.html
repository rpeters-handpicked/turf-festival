Jana Kovač

About

What does nightlife look like in 2030? In this keynote, we explore how club culture, electronic music and urban policy are shaping the future of cities — and how cities in turn reshape the sounds that emerge from them. From Amsterdam's 24-hour venue licenses to Berlin's cultural preservation battles, we map the fault lines between creative freedom and institutional pressure.

Speakers draw on field research from 12 European cities to ask: who gets to define what nightlife is for? And what happens when the dancefloor becomes a site of political resistance? Expect honest conversation, no easy answers, and a lot of bass frequency references.
